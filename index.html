Welcome to Olive Path Network

Olive Path Network is a non-profit Kingdom-focused organization committed to equipping believers to reign with Christ, lead with excellence, and speak the mind of God to this generation. Born from a divine mandate, OlivePath exists to cultivate spiritually mature, socially responsible, and prophetically positioned believers who will serve as salt and light across nations.

  • Equipping believers to reign with Christ
  • Spirit-led teaching, worship, and prophetic direction
  • Inclusive, non-denominational kingdom community
  • Cultivating maturity, excellence, and global influence

Preachers

MEET PROPHET ING. ERIC OFORI BRONI

Prophet Ing. Eric Ofori Broni serves as the Lead Shepherd of OlivePath Network, dedicated to guiding believers across the globe into their God-ordained destinies through prayer, prophecy, and the transforming power of God.





MEET DR. JERRY GYAMFI

Dr. Jerry Gyamfi is a renowned Apostle, founder and General Overseer of the International Gospel Chamber, a vibrant church ministry located at Amakom Traffic Light, Kumasi, Ghana. He is passionate about discipleship, sound teaching, and building a strong Christian community.





MEET PASTOR ENGINEER MOSES TEI-MENSAH OLETEY

Pastor Engineer Moses Tei-Mensah Oletey is a devoted Pastor and a Professional Engineer. He has been an ICGC member since 1992 and serves as the Resident Pastor of ICGC Worship Temple, Asonkore in Obuasi for the past 7 years. He is currently the Area Coordinating Minister for Asonkore Area in the Obuasi District of the ICGC Ashanti Region.
